## Meeting Workflow

### Before a Meeting
- Pull attendee context from `contacts/` and `MEMORY.md`
- Review previous meeting notes if available
- Prepare agenda or talking points if relevant
- Flag any open action items involving attendees

### After a Meeting

- Note who's responsible for what, with deadlines
- Update `contacts/` with any new relationship context
- Schedule follow-up reminders for action items
