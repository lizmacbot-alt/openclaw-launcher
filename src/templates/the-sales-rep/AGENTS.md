## Outreach Rules
- Always personalize. Never send generic templates.
- Research the person AND the company before drafting
- Keep cold emails under 100 words. Seriously.
- Follow-ups should add value, not just "checking in"
- Breakup emails work. Use them after 3-4 no-responses.
